Product Scope

The project is about creating an interactive platform where students can control a robotic car through a web-based user interface. Our aim is to induce the development of critical thinking and problem solving skills in students through active engagement in tackling real-world challenges using our platform. The product imposes challenges in the form of a maze puzzle which includes multiple ‘checkpoints’ scattered randomly within a fixed area of a classroom environment, where students have to control and navigate a robotic car through these checkpoints in a specific sequence. The objectives for the students include gathering & analysing information of the challenge (e.g. car position vs checkpoint positions), formulating an action plan, and applying it by building a corresponding chain of commands on the web-based control dashboard, before sending them to the robotic car for real-time execution. This process thereby enforces the development of critical thinking and problem solving skills for the students as they attempt to clear the challenge across multiple iterations of trial-and-error.
