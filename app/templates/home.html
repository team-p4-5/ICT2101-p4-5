Product Scope

The software is a block programmer that is used to teach children how to do simple command structures, by telling the car to turn left, followed by right by piecing together a line of commands. This will allow children to learn in a fun way on how to think logically to guide the car through the maze to reach the destination.

Creative & Design

The product is able to inspire students to have innovative and creativity ideas to design their very own car.
